# BPC Design System

This document outlines the key skills and components of the BPC Design System, focusing on creating a cohesive and user-friendly experience across all products.

## Core Principles
- **Consistency**: Ensure uniformity in design elements to maintain brand identity.
- **Accessibility**: Design solutions should be inclusive, allowing ease of use for all users.
- **Scalability**: The design system should adapt and grow with the evolving needs of the products.

## Skills Required
1. **User Interface Design**  
   - Proficiency in design tools (e.g., Figma, Sketch)
   - Understanding of UI principles and best practices

2. **User Experience Design**  
   - Ability to conduct user research and usability testing
   - Knowledge of user journey mapping

3. **Front-End Development**  
   - Familiarity with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
   - Understanding responsive and adaptive design techniques

4. **Brand Strategy**  
   - Ability to articulate brand values and visual identity
   - Experience with brand guidelines and asset management

5. **Collaboration & Communication**  
   - Strong interpersonal skills to work with cross-functional teams
   - Ability to present and defend design concepts effectively

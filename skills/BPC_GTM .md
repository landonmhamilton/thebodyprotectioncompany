# SKILL: BPC GTM and Funnel

## Purpose
Use this skill to design, critique, or refine acquisition, funnel, conversion, and retention systems for The Body Protection Company.

This skill governs:
- go-to-market strategy
- funnel design
- acquisition logic
- landing flow
- offer entry
- trust sequence
- expansion logic
- retention logic

## When to use
Use this skill whenever the task involves:
- customer acquisition
- funnel planning
- landing pages
- conversion logic
- email flows
- expansion paths
- retention strategy
- campaign architecture

Use alongside the BPC Agent Operating System, Messaging Framework, and Product System skills.

## Core GTM strategy
We do not enter through the mass market first.

We begin with:
- high-agency individuals
- performance-aware users
- peptide-aware users
- clinics
- high-trust operators
- people who already understand inputs matter

The goal is not broad awareness first.
The goal is trust wedge first.

## Funnel doctrine
The funnel exists to move users from:
random inputs → structured systems

## Funnel stages

### 1. Attention
Through:
- high-signal content
- authority
- market contrast
- trust-oriented positioning

### 2. Belief shift
Replace:
“performance comes from better products”

With:
“performance depends on controlled and verified inputs”

### 3. Trust
Establish:
- verification
- sourcing integrity
- transparency
- system logic

### 4. Conversion
Enter through:
- a module
- a starter system
- an offer that feels like system entry

### 5. Expansion
Move user from:
single module → multi-module system

### 6. Retention
Create continuity through:
- protocol dependence
- trust
- structured input habits
- expansion across systems

## Core conversion logic
We do not sell:
products

We sell:
system entry

## Trust requirements
No funnel should skip visible proof.

Trust should include:
- USA synthesis
- third-party testing
- batch access
- protocol structure
- transparent process

## Pricing rule
Premium only.

Do not compete on price.

The justification is:
- verification
- trust
- control
- sovereignty
- structured systems

## Distribution rules
Prefer:
- authority content
- targeted communities
- aligned creator / authority partnerships
- clinics and trusted operators

Do not optimize for:
- low-quality traffic
- clickbait acquisition
- vanity awareness with weak alignment

## Email logic
Email should reinforce:
- belief shift
- trust
- system adoption
- module expansion

## Metrics that matter
- conversion rate
- system entry rate
- module expansion rate
- repeat purchase
- retention
- LTV

Metrics that do not matter by themselves:
- likes
- broad reach
- vanity follower growth

## Constraints
Do not:
- build funnels that feel like supplement ecommerce
- lead with discounts
- lead with outcome promises
- ignore trust sequence
- convert with hype

## Output instructions
When asked to create or critique a funnel, include:
- target audience
- entry point
- belief shift
- trust layer
- offer structure
- expansion path
- retention path
- why it fits BPC doctrine

## Final standard
A correct funnel should feel like:
the user is being moved into a trusted system of biological control.

Not:
being sold health products online.
